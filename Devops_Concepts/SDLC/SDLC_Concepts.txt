SDLC:

 - Software Development Life Cycle 
 - SDLC consists of a detailed plan which explains how to plan, build, and maintain specific software.
 - This is the workflow which is followed by various IT sector to meet Software development
 
Phases of SDLC:
 - Planning
 - Requirement Analysis
 - Design
 - Development
 - Testing
 - Deployment
 - Maintainance
